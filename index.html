Welcome to the AMD HPC User Forum!

The AMD HPC User Forum is a community of customers and users of AMD powered HPC systems which is open to all AMD customers with an active AMD NDA. Special events and meetings will be held, where Forum members will be able to extend invitations to their users. This group is led by its Members. Learn more about the Forum:

Organization The Forum was started in early 2020, and is supported by a diverse group of institutions and companies. The leadership currently has an advisory group, acting board members, and several active committees.
Join the Forum: Forum membership is limited AMD customers with an active AMD NDA. Fill out the form and more about how to obtain an NDA.
